Rules:
~ The app will request for the user's location, allowing the hunt to take place with a live tracking feature,
making sure the players are at the correct location at any given time.

~ The app will provide questions, and the players should reply with answers - one at a time

~ The questions will be provided in different formats; including True/False, multiple choice, text/number input, and more.
Note that the textual and numerical input are case-sensitive!

~ Correct answers will reward the player with points, while wrong answers will subtract points.
The amount of points earned or deducted varies depending on the type of question.

~ Players can skip questions, however points will be deducted. Some questions cannot be skipped.

~ A time limit will be given, players can check the remaining time at any given moment.
Players can also access a leaderboard to check their standing against other teams.

PLAY!
